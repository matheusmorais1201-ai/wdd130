São Paulo - Brazil

São Paulo is the capital of the Brazilian state of São Paulo. Classified by the Globalization and World Cities Research Network (GaWC) as an alpha global city , it is the most populous urban area in the world outside of Asia. São Paulo is the largest urban economy in Latin America and one of the world's major financial centres, representing around 10% of the Brazilian GDP and just over a third of São Paulo state's GDP (the only Brazilian city to surpass the 1 trillion reais mark in GDP).
